\section{Question 5}
$$
\left\lbrace \begin{array}{cl}
    \dot{x}_1(t)  =& x_2(t)\\[1em]
    \dot{x}_2(t)  =& -\omega_n^2x_1(t) - 2\zeta\omega_nx_2(t) + w(t)
    \end{array}\right.
$$
where $\omega_n = 10$, $\zeta = 0.3$ and $w(t)$ is with power $\sigma^2$ and step time $0.01_{\sec}$ ($\sigma^2 = 1$).
